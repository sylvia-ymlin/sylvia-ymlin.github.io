A New Beginning

Hello, world. Welcome to my little corner of the internet.

This is the first post on this site, and as is tradition, I'll use it to introduce myself and this space.

My name is Fitz (Yangmei Lin). I'm a developer who loves building things that are useful, reading about ideas that are strange, and wandering to places that are beautiful.

Why a Personal Site?

I've been meaning to have a personal website for a while. Not just a portfolio — but a place. Somewhere to think in public, share what I'm learning, document what I'm building.

The internet is full of noise. But personal sites have always felt different — quieter, more intentional. Like someone's living room instead of a shopping mall.

So here we are.

What to Expect

I'll be writing about:

  • Code — technical deep dives, project notes, things I learned the hard way
  • Life — coffee, books, travel, random observations
  • Ideas — things I'm thinking about, questions without clear answers

The site is bilingual — some posts in English, some in Chinese (中文), some in both. Write the language you think in, I suppose.

开始

你好。欢迎来到这里。

这是我个人网站的第一篇文章。用中文写一段，算是个仪式感的开始。

希望这里能成为一个真实的地方——记录思考，分享探索，偶尔说些无关紧要却想说的话。

See you in the next one. 👋
